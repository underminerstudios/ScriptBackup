Optimize MultiSubMaterials
author: Norman Schaar (norman.schaar@gameloft.com)
  • This script will do the following on the materials of the selected objects:
  • Step 1: Find redundant instanced submaterials and collapse them.
  • Step 2: Internal sorting of submaterials.
  • Step 3: Delete unused submaterials.
  • Step 4: Reorder material IDs and polygon IDs to compress the multisub material.
