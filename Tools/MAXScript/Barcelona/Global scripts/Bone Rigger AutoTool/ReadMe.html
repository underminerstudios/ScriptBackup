Bone-rigger Autotool
author: Marc Marti - marc.marti@gameloft.com
DESCRIPTION:
  • This script will allow you to rig automatically Physics MassFx sets and cloth sets. The script will create the necessary bones for each of the pieces: A single bone for each riggid-body object in a riggid-body MassFx simulation. A bone for each vertex in a cloth simulation.
INSTRUCTIONS:
  • Prepare your MassFx riggid body or Cloth simulation.
  • Click the proper Rig button (Cloth or MassFx).
KNOWN ISSUES:
  • Due the nature of the script and the expensive cost fo the realtime skinning, avoid creating too complex simulations that will require a lot of bones. (This tends to happen quite easily with the cloth simulations)
